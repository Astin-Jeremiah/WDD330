Mobile First Development Notes

  • Whatever you are designing do mobile first.
  • Mobile first does not equal responsive retrofitting your standard site.
  • Be There...Be Useful...Be Quick
  • Mobile users usually use their thumbs and operate the device with one hand.
  • What is the easies you can make a mobile request form?
    • Use Proper Keyboards
    • Use Proper Labels
    • Use Autofill
